Running Asian Coffee

When I was a high school student, I made my own coffee shop in my school and ran it. I did it because the original school store had been closed for a while due to COVID19. Aside from coffee, I sold some Japanese things like matcha, miso-soup. That place was beloved by lots of students. I even made official web application for it.(it's not opened anymore.)

Brief explanations of Asian coffee apps' function

With the official web app of Asian coffee, you can do online ordering and register yoursefl for memebership for asian coffee. For user registration, I used allauth, and for online purchasing I used stripe.
